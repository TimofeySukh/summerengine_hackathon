# Heat Wave

Small first-person katana combat slice in a stylized Japanese-inspired mountain grove.

The player starts surrounded by hostile bots and survives by closing distance, dodging, jumping, and cutting enemies down with a katana.

## Current Prototype

- Base: Summer `3d-third-person-controller` template.
- View: first-person camera mounted at player head height.
- Movement: WASD, mouse look, Space jump.
- Combat: left mouse performs the katana/melee attack.
- Enemies: ground beetle bots and flying bee bots placed around the start area.
- Economy: coin drops and coin UI are disabled for this slice.
- Assets: CC0 low-poly katana model (`player/katana/`). Reused template environment, enemies, sounds, UI, and VFX.
