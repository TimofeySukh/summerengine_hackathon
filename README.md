# Heat Wave

First-person katana combat prototype in a flat night-city arena inspired by cyberpunk Japan.

The player starts on a concrete arena surrounded by dark city blocks, neon light, fog, and humanoid chasers. Enemies spawn over time, walk toward the player, and deal contact damage.

## Controls

- `WASD`: move
- `Mouse`: look
- `Left mouse`: katana attack
- `Space`: jump
- `Esc`: pause

Katana attacks use a first-person hit ray plus a short-range slash volume.

## Base Template

Built from the Summer Engine `3d-third-person-controller` template, reworked into first-person melee combat with a flatter custom arena and simple humanoid chaser enemies.
